Opinel Tale

In this Blender animation, you can witness an absurd story of a round-ended Opinel knife on a quest to gain strength. The animation follows the Opinel on his weight-lifting journet, aimed at achieving its goal of slicing through a loaf of bread. This animation plays with anthropomorphism, turning the inanimate knife into a character with ambition. As the knife finally succeeds in cutting a slice, the scene captures both the triumph and the lighthearted spirit of the story. This short animation uses 3D modeling on Blender to weave a narrative of perseverance and success.
